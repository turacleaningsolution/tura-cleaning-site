About Us

TURA Cleaning Solutions is a mobile cleaning unit based in Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea. We specialize in residential and commercial cleaning, offering a wide range of services from carpet and deep cleaning to post-construction cleanups. Co-owned and operated by a passionate husband and wife team, we are committed to delivering quality, reliability, and transformational results.
