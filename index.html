About Us

TURA CLEANING SOLUTIONS is a mobile cleaning unit based in Port Moresby, PNG. We specialize in residential and commercial cleaning services including carpet care, post-construction cleaning, yard cleanup, and sanitation spray-downs. Our mission is to not just clean — but transform spaces.

Our Services

  • 🧼 Standard Cleaning – Surface cleaning, dusting, mopping & sanitizing.
  • 🏠 Deep Cleaning – Thorough top-to-bottom cleaning for all spaces.
  • 🧯 Disinfection – Virus-safe disinfection with hospital-grade solutions.
  • 🚪 Move In/Out Cleaning – Perfect for end-of-lease or new tenant prep.
  • 🏗️ Post-Construction – Clear debris, dust, and polish newly built or renovated spaces.
  • 🌿 Yard & Outdoor Cleaning – Sweep, remove debris, trim grass & clean driveways.
